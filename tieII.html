Tie II by Andrew Nguyen
Boys seem to like to claim
That women tie them down.
As if having close ties
With someone is such a bad thing.
However there’s a difference
Between Boys and Men.
Boys wear
White t-shirts,
Cargo pants,
And sneakers.
Men wear
Dress shoes,
Dress pants,
And a dress shirt
Strangled in a clean tie.
When shoelaces become loose
Boys are too selfish
To tend to the knot.
When ties become loose
Men simply
Straighten out the knot.
Boys
Tend to loosen their knots
As soon as they’re ‘tied down’
And just as quickly
Tie another knot
With someone else
Not too far away.
You see,
Boys have a
Commitment problem.
Men
Do Knot.
