Tie I by Andrew Nguyen
With so many close ties
It’s impossible to keep
All of your close ties tight
As more ties become tied
In this ocean of rope
It’s easy
For ties to tangle,
For ties to loosen,
For ties to become lost in the vastness
Especially if
The rope is long
The distance is hard to make up for
Dear friend,
Just because our ties have loosened
It doesn’t mean that
Our ties are any less precious to me
I may lose sight of our ties
But as long as the rope is still there
If the distance is ever closed
I hope our ends meet again
So we can tighten our knot
