Beauty by Andrew Nguyen
A universal understanding of what beauty is
Is as ambiguous as water.
If beauty is defined as the content of one’s character,
Then water is a vast cyan body that kisses the sun twice a day.
If beauty is simply a checklist of physical appearances,
Then water is a shallow pool.
It’s easy to dive incorrectly into the dating game
And break your neck.
But if your beauty is like an ocean,
Then dating would be like deep sea excavations.
Every new fish would be a new adventure
It wouldn’t be hard finding other fish in this sea,
If your eyes can truly sea beauty.
